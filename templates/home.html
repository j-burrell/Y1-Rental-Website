Information

Our cozy and stylish 2-bedroom apartment features modern Scandi decor, high-speed WiFi and all the home comforts you need to feel welcome! Perfect for short breaks, family getaways and business or study stays. Maximum occupancy is 6 people and we have a small on-site parking space. Set in the award-winning Norwich Lanes area in the city centre, you’ll find tourist attractions, shopping, arts venues, fine restaurants and excellent cafes all within a few minutes’ walk. See below for more details.

Rooms

Living and Dining Room

With gorgeous cathedral views, this open-plan room consists of a living room, dining area, and attached kitchen. The lounge has a corner sofa (that becomes a double bed in one swift motion), an armchair, a coffee table, and a flat-screen TV with 85+ channels plus in-built casting and streaming capabilities. The dining area has a space-saving table and four chairs, which can also be used as a workspace, and some storage shelves with board games and books.

Kitchen

This area has a small fridge-freezer, sink and drainer, washer/dryer, iron, oven with gas stove and hood, kettle, toaster, and a Nespresso coffee machine (please note there is no microwave). It's fully equipped with crockery, pots and pans, cutlery and basic cooking items like oil, herbs, and spices.

Bathroom

This room contains a shower/bath combo, a toilet, a sink unit, a mirrored cupboard, and a heated towel rail. We provide shampoo, shower gel and hand soap, and towels are included for the number of guests in your booking.

Upstairs Bedroom

A stylish, unique and spacious bedroom with high ceilings and a very tall window. This bedroom is located on the upstairs level and includes a sumptuous super king-size bed (200cm x 180cm), a clothing rail and some drawer/shelf space, bedside tables, lamps, and plugs with USB charging points by the bedside. It also has a laptop-friendly workspace, and a flat-screen TV with an HDMI cable and a Chromecast to watch BBC iPlayer, Netflix, YouTube from your phone/tablet/laptop (please note this TV has no access to live channels).

Downstairs Bedroom

A lovely double room with big windows and lots of light. This bedroom is located on the downstairs floor of the apartment and includes a double bed, a wardrobe for clothes and other essentials, a full-length mirror, bedside tables, lamps, and plugs with USB charging points by the bedside.

Outside Space & Parking

There's a courtyard featuring benches and bicycle racks which is shared with our neighbours, and there's also an underground car park on-site. One parking space is available for you to use free of charge (please note that it's quite small; if you have a large vehicle, get in touch so we can share other options). While we don't have a garden ourselves, we are just a stone's throw from the beautiful public park Chapelfield Gardens.

Amenities

The home comes with the following: Wireless Internet, Cable TV, Dryer, Iron, Television, Essential Kit, Heating, Washer, Laptop Friendly Workspace, Hair Dryer, Bed and Bath Supplies.
